Incarceration by Race and Gender over Time

Rebecca Chan

2/25/2022

In this report, I focused on race and gender of incarcerated populations in the US. The latest population data came from 2016, when there was a total of 518147 incarcerated indivduals in the US. In 2016, the average percentage of women in prison was 8.46% and the average percentage of men was 91.54%. Over time there has been an average change of 6.18 for women and 61.74 for men.
